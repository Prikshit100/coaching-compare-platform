📖 About Allen Career Institute

Allen Career Institute, founded in 1988 in Kota, Rajasthan, is one of India's most prestigious coaching institutes for competitive exam preparation. With over 35 years of excellence, Allen has helped lakhs of students achieve their dreams of getting into IITs, AIIMS, and other premier institutions.

The institute is known for its rigorous academic curriculum, experienced faculty members, and a highly structured approach to exam preparation. Allen's study material is considered among the best in the industry and its mock test series closely mirrors actual exam patterns.
