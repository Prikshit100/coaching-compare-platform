❓ Frequently Asked
How do I list my institute? ▼
Click "List Your Institute" on the homepage and fill the registration form. Our team verifies your documents within 24–48 hours before your listing goes live.
Is Classify free for students? ▼
Yes, completely free! Students can browse, compare, read reviews and apply to institutes without paying anything. We earn from institutes, not students.
How does commission work? ▼
Institutes pay Classify a small commission only when a student they received through Classify actually enrolls. You only pay when we deliver real results.
How do I report wrong information? ▼
Use the contact form and select "Report incorrect information" as your subject. Our team will verify and update the info within 48 hours.
Can I delete a review about my institute? ▼
We do not delete reviews unless they violate our content policy (fake, abusive, or spam). Contact us with proof and we'll investigate within 5 working days.
